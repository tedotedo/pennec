About the network

The Paediatric Epilepsy Network for the North East and Cumbria (PENNEC) is a key player in the UK's epilepsy care network. We provide specialist care and coordinated management for children and young people with epilepsy across our region.

Our aim is to improve diagnosis and treatment for different seizure types and epilepsy syndromes, reduce risks for people with epilepsy, and share pockets of excellent practice so every family benefits.

PENNEC sits within the broader CHWN (Children's Health and Wellbeing Network), supporting the delivery of the Children and Young Person's Transformation Agenda and service improvement for CYP with long-term conditions, including epilepsy.

What we do

  • ◆
    Share best practice

    Identifying and spreading pockets of excellence across trusts in the region.

  • ◆
    Support better diagnosis & treatment

    Across seizure types and epilepsy syndromes, through shared learning and audit.

  • ◆
    Reduce risk

    Promoting safety, SUDEP awareness and robust transition practice.

  • ◆
    Convene the network

    Regular meetings, education and collaboration for professionals working in CYP epilepsy care.
