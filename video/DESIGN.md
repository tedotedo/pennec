# PENNEC — Visual Identity

## Style Prompt
Warm, editorial, human. A paediatric epilepsy network for the North East & Cumbria — the tone is confident but never clinical-cold. Cream canvas with deep navy typography and a single amber accent that behaves like late-afternoon light. Soft radial glows, hairline rules, and slow ambient drift. No sterile blues, no neon, no gradient text. Think a good charity annual report or a Wellcome Collection poster — trustworthy and kind.

## Colors
- Canvas cream — `#fbf6ec`
- Warm off-cream — `#f3ecdc`
- Deep navy (primary ink) — `#0f2a44`
- Navy hover — `#1a3a5c`
- Amber accent (single accent) — `#f4c05e`
- Amber deep — `#d89c2e`
- Coral (sparingly, emphasis) — `#e8765b`

## Typography
- Display: **Fraunces** 700 (opsz high, for poster weight in titles and stats)
- UI / body: **Inter** 400–600

## Motion
- 0.5–0.9s entrances, vary eases (`power3.out`, `expo.out`, `back.out(1.2)`)
- Slow ambient drift on decoratives (6–10s cycles)
- Stagger type reveals 80–120ms
- No exits between scenes — transitions carry the cut

## What NOT to Do
- No gradient text
- No cyan / purple / neon accents
- No pure `#000` or `#fff` — always cream/navy
- No cold blues, no stethoscope-stock-photo clichés
- No everything-centered equal-weight layouts
